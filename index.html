Why does car insurance differ by State to State?
There are many factors that affect the difference in cost of car insurance per state. Such differences include factors such as the population density of the state, road conditions, number of licensed drivers, cost of living as it relates to repair and labor costs, and the percent of uninsured drivers in the area.
